VirtualBiotech is a non-commercial initiative managed by venture capital professionals with the aim to form a free online community for life science investors in Europe.

VirtualBiotech is NOT a fund raising platform. We solely enable scientists and life science enterpreneurs to introduce their projects/companies to our community members.

See how you can benefit from Virtual Biotech

For life science investors:

  • Browse the listed investment opportunities and stay up to date with the life science startup scene in Europe
  • Download the available slide decks freely and anonymously and move any time to direct contact with the founders
  • Find out which projects are most popular in the community and have the choice to express your interest in any listed project
  • By expressing interest, you will be introduced to other interested members to potentially share your due diligence and build syndicates

Join our investor community »

For life science entrepreneurs:

  • Benefit from our community to reach more life science investors, pave the way for future contact or follow up in a soft and indirect way
  • List your company or project simply and without any commitment to our platform
  • Your company will be added to our deal flow which will be sent to our community members of life science investors
  • The investors will be able to check your company profile, contact you directly and share their interest with other investors
